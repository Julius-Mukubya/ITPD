## Content Specifications

### Article Requirements
- **Length:** 800-1,500 words
- **Reading Time:** 5-10 minutes
- **Format:** Well-structured with headers, bullet points, and actionable advice
- **Tone:** Supportive, non-judgmental, evidence-based
- **Include:** Statistics, expert quotes, practical tips, resources

### Video Requirements
- **Length:** 3-8 minutes
- **Format:** Educational, testimonial, or instructional
- **Quality:** Professional audio, clear visuals
- **Accessibility:** Closed captions, transcripts
- **Include:** Call-to-action for further resources

### Infographic Requirements
- **Format:** Vertical (Pinterest-style) or horizontal
- **Content:** Data visualization, step-by-step guides, quick reference
- **Design:** Accessible colors, readable fonts, mobile-friendly
- **Include:** Source citations, contact information

### Document Requirements
- **Format:** PDF, easily downloadable
- **Content:** Comprehensive guides, resource lists, emergency procedures
- **Length:** 2-10 pages depending on topic
- **Include:** Contact information, additional resources

## Content Guidelines

### Tone and Voice
- **Supportive and non-judgmental**
- **Evidence-based and factual**
- **Accessible to college-age audience**
- **Hopeful and empowering**
- **Culturally sensitive and inclusive**

### Key Messages
- Recovery is possible
- Seeking help is a sign of strength
- You are not alone
- Small steps lead to big changes
- Resources are available and accessible

### Compliance Requirements
- **Medical accuracy:** All health information reviewed by qualified professionals
- **Legal compliance:** Accurate information about laws and policies
- **Privacy protection:** No identifying information in testimonials
- **Crisis resources:** Always include emergency contact information
- **Accessibility:** Content accessible to users with disabilities

## Legal and Ethical Considerations

### Content Review Process
1. **Medical/Clinical Review:** All health content reviewed by qualified professionals
2. **Legal Review:** Policy and legal information verified
3. **Accessibility Review:** Content tested for accessibility compliance
4. **Student Review:** Feedback from target audience
5. **Final Approval:** Administrative sign-off before publication

### Privacy and Consent
- Written consent for all testimonials and personal stories
- No identifying information without explicit permission
- Clear privacy policies for user-generated content
- Secure handling of sensitive information

### Crisis Content Guidelines
- Always include crisis resources and contact information
- Clear disclaimers about emergency situations
- Professional review of all crisis-related content
- Regular updates to resource contact information
